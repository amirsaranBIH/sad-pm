About Sad.pm

SadPM is a project created by Mystie to create a free space where people who suffer from depression, anxiety and more are able to receive quick and helpful emotional support or help.

The aim of said project is to be able to both spread awareness of depression and anxiety, along with the ability to be by the side of people who are fighting them. We also want to stress that we are here for anything. With the right tools and resources, we aim to help as many people as possible.

Volunteering

We're always looking for new members of the team. If you would like to help us out, please E-mail me or Jago. If you do apply to become part of the team, however, you must be willing to respond as quickly as possible.

We hope that we can eventually be able to help as many people as possible, and volunteering makes this possible. We are also looking for web designers and graphic designers, so please also E-mail us if you work within those professions.
